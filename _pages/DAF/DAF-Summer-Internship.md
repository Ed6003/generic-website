# Key Contributions

- Developed a **MATLAB app** freely distributed to over **130 DAF UK dealerships** as a proof-of-concept.
- Implemented **graph theory** for optimal HGV vehicle routing with max speed, vehicle size and border limits.
- Integrated **GIS data** from imported or generated routes to fetch real-time simulation parameters with **API’s**.
- Worked in the **prototyping and R&D area** preparing **BEV trucks** for pavé and destructive testing.
- Presented project to senior management with a **live demo** and discussed commercialisation of the app.
